Legacy of the Sharingan

I’m Joshua Sharingan, son of Jagan Sharingan, known across Singanallur as "Singai Ja"—a name spoken with both fear and reverence. My father was a legend, his Sharingan unmatched, but it came with a price. Before he fell protecting our village, he passed his eyes to me, calling it both a gift and a burden.

On my first mission, I began to understand his warning. In the forest, the air grew heavy, alive with an ancient energy. A masked shinobi appeared, his Sharingan burning brighter than mine.

“Son of Singai Ja,” he sneered.“Do you know the cost of your father’s power?”.

I attacked, but he was faster. His Sharingan pierced my mind, dragging me into visions of my father’s last stand. Blood. Fire. His voice echoed: “Joshua, rise above the pain. Protect them.”

When I broke free, my Sharingan had changed—evolving into the Mangekyō Sharingan. Power surged through me as I trapped him in my Genjutsu, leaving him broken.

As I walked away, his words lingered:“The Sharingan owns you now, as it did your father.”

I carry my father’s legacy, determined to protect the people of Singanallur. But sometimes, I wonder… how much of myself these eyes will take before it’s over.
